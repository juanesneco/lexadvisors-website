Our Services

Every case is unique and we treat it as such. Please ask for an informative session with us in order to review the possibility of allowing us to help you.

Real Estate

Lex Advisors is a pioneer in high-end Real Estate Law in Mexico, having helped close over 3,000 properties and $3B+ in transactions. We provide comprehensive legal aid for real estate transactions, due diligence, and title searches, always with reliability and transparency.

Notary Services

Our founding partner is a certified Federal Notary and active alternate State Notary. We facilitate all State and Federal Notary Services, essential for successful legal acts in Mexico.

Corporate Services

We represent parties in complex corporate processes, including entity creation, transformation, dissolution, and liquidation. Our services cover mergers, acquisitions, due diligence, and regulatory compliance.

Appraisals

We provide expert appraisals for real estate, businesses, corporations, and commercial assets. Our appraisers are recognized nationally and internationally for their expertise.

Tax Law

Our team helps clients navigate complex tax systems, offering expertise in tax treaties, FATCA, CRS, and BEPS measures for cross-border compliance and optimal business results.

Rights & Entitlement

We have an unmatched record in securing timely governmental permit approvals for development projects in Baja. Our experience spans all types of land use and entitlement legal services.

Employment Law

We provide comprehensive labour law services, including litigation, corporate restructurings, and human rights law. We assist both employers and employees in planning and resolving disputes.

Construction & Development

We are experts in legal structure and logistics for real estate and business developments, ensuring efficient coordination and compliance throughout all project phases.

Compliance Anti-Money Laundering

Our compliance department specializes in anti-money laundering for real estate, art, jewelry, and civil associations. We guide clients through registration, operational manuals, and regulatory advisory.

Title Search

We offer deep title searches, essential for minimizing liabilities and securing title insurance. Our team is endorsed by major title insurance companies in North America.

Mediation & Conciliation

We believe in non-adversarial solutions. Our certified mediators and federal arbitrators provide fair, professional mediation and alternative dispute resolution services.

Note: We do not do litigation in any legal matter other than in Employment Law.
