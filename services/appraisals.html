Services / Appraisals

Lex Advisors provides expert appraisal services for real estate and commercial assets, delivering accurate valuations that meet legal and financial requirements.

Our appraisal team includes certified appraisers and legal professionals who understand both the market dynamics and legal requirements for property valuations in Mexico. We provide comprehensive appraisal reports that are accepted by banks, courts, and government agencies.

We specialize in residential properties, commercial real estate, land valuations, and business asset appraisals. Our appraisals are conducted using industry-standard methodologies and include detailed market analysis, comparable sales, and legal considerations.

Whether you need an appraisal for purchase, sale, financing, insurance, or legal proceedings, our team provides reliable, defensible valuations that protect your interests and meet all regulatory requirements.

With 25+ years of experience in the Baja California real estate market, Lex Advisors has established a reputation for accurate, professional appraisals that clients can trust for their most important financial decisions.
