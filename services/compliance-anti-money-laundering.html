Services / Compliance & Anti-Money Laundering

Lex Advisors provides comprehensive compliance and anti-money laundering services to help businesses meet regulatory requirements and protect against financial crime risks.

Our compliance practice specializes in anti-money laundering for real estate, art, jewelry, and civil associations. We guide clients through registration processes, operational manual development, and regulatory advisory to ensure full compliance.

We assist with AML program development, risk assessments, compliance monitoring, and regulatory reporting. Our team ensures that all compliance programs meet Mexican regulatory requirements and international standards.

Whether you're establishing a new business, updating compliance programs, or facing regulatory scrutiny, our compliance services provide the expertise and guidance you need to maintain regulatory compliance.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican regulatory compliance, Lex Advisors has helped hundreds of businesses establish and maintain effective compliance programs, ensuring regulatory compliance and risk mitigation.
