Corporate Services

Services / Corporate Services

We have experience representing parties in some of the most complex and significant corporate processes in the history of the Baja.

As a complement to our other services, we are capable of creating, transforming, dissolving, and liquidating legal entities. We are also capable of aiding with the formalization of assembly meetings and other more complex corporate actions. Having assisted with important corporate transactions, we are familiar and comfortable with the undertaking of mergers and acquisitions and with the provision of a legal due diligence service.

The law changes its requisites every day, and that has clear consequences. We help clients understand how to mitigate risks and know what needs to be done in order to comply with the law without necessarily giving up other key business areas.

Other of our corporate services are:

  • Secretarial Advisory Services
  • Governing and Managing Body
  • Shareholder Conflicts
  • Regulatory Reporting
  • Corporate compliance
