Corporate Services

Services / Corporate Services

Lex Advisors provides comprehensive corporate legal services to help businesses establish, operate, and grow successfully in Mexico.

Our corporate services include entity formation, corporate restructuring, mergers and acquisitions, corporate governance, and regulatory compliance. We work with both domestic and international companies to ensure their corporate structure meets all legal requirements.

We assist with business registration, corporate documentation, shareholder agreements, and board governance. Our team ensures that all corporate actions comply with Mexican corporate law and international standards.

Whether you're starting a new business, expanding operations, or restructuring existing entities, our corporate services provide the legal foundation and strategic guidance you need for success in the Mexican market.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican corporate law, Lex Advisors has helped hundreds of businesses establish and maintain their corporate presence in Mexico, ensuring compliance, efficiency, and long-term success.
