Services / Rights & Entitlement

Lex Advisors has an unmatched record in securing timely governmental permit approvals for development projects throughout Baja California.

Our entitlement services cover all types of land use and development projects, including residential, commercial, mixed-use, and industrial developments. We navigate the complex Mexican regulatory framework to ensure your project receives all necessary approvals.

We specialize in zoning changes, environmental permits, construction permits, and land use entitlements. Our team works closely with government agencies and regulatory bodies to expedite the approval process.

Whether you're developing a residential community, commercial property, or industrial facility, our entitlement services provide the expertise and relationships needed to secure all necessary permits and approvals.

With 25+ years of experience in Baja California development, Lex Advisors has helped hundreds of projects secure their entitlements, ensuring timely approvals and successful project completion.
