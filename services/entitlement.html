Services / Rights & Entitlement

Lex Advisors specializes in rights and entitlement services, helping clients secure the necessary permits and approvals for their projects and business operations in Mexico.

Our entitlement practice covers governmental permit acquisition, zoning approvals, environmental clearances, and regulatory compliance. We work with federal, state, and local government agencies to ensure efficient approval processes.

We assist with land use permits, development approvals, environmental impact assessments, and regulatory filings. Our team ensures that all applications comply with Mexican regulations and meet government requirements for timely approval.

Whether you're developing property, starting a business, or expanding operations, our rights and entitlement services provide the legal expertise and government relations you need to secure necessary approvals efficiently.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican regulatory processes, Lex Advisors has helped hundreds of clients successfully navigate the complex approval landscape, ensuring timely permits and regulatory compliance.
