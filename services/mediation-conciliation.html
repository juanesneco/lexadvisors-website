Services / Mediation & Conciliation

Lex Advisors provides professional mediation and conciliation services to help parties resolve disputes through non-adversarial, collaborative processes.

Our mediation practice includes certified mediators and federal arbitrators who provide fair, professional mediation and alternative dispute resolution services. We believe in non-adversarial solutions that preserve relationships and achieve mutually beneficial outcomes.

We specialize in commercial disputes, real estate conflicts, employment matters, and family business disputes. Our mediators use proven techniques and methodologies to facilitate productive dialogue and reach sustainable agreements.

Whether you're facing a business dispute, real estate conflict, or employment issue, our mediation services provide the neutral, professional guidance you need to resolve conflicts efficiently and cost-effectively.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican legal practice, Lex Advisors has successfully mediated hundreds of disputes, helping parties achieve mutually beneficial resolutions and preserve valuable relationships.
