Notary Services

Services / Notary Services

Our founding partner was, at the moment of his licensing examination, the youngest certified Federal Notary of the country. He is also, simultaneously, an active alternate State Notary.

A Notary Public is vital for the successful execution of many legal acts in Mexico. It is important for foreigners to understand that these individuals are essential in the daily civil, business and corporate initiatives. In Mexico, a Notary Public is a highly qualified attorney that has satisfactorily surpassed numerous formal legal qualifications.

We are able to facilitate provide all State and Federal Notary Services, ensuring that all your legal documents are properly authenticated and legally binding.

Whether you need document authentication, contract notarization, or legal certification, our notary services provide the legal foundation and peace of mind you require for your transactions in Mexico.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican legal procedures, Lex Advisors has established strong relationships with reputable notaries throughout Baja California, ensuring efficient and reliable service for all your notarial needs.
