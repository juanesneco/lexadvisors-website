Services / Tax Law

Lex Advisors provides comprehensive tax law services to help individuals and businesses navigate the complex Mexican tax system and international tax obligations.

Our tax services include tax planning, compliance, international tax treaties, FATCA compliance, CRS reporting, and BEPS implementation. We help clients understand and comply with Mexican tax laws and international tax obligations.

We specialize in cross-border transactions, international tax planning, transfer pricing, and tax dispute resolution. Our team ensures that clients meet all filing requirements and regulatory obligations while optimizing their tax position.

Whether you're an individual with international income, a business expanding into Mexico, or dealing with complex tax structures, our tax services provide the expertise and guidance you need for compliance and optimization.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican and international tax law, Lex Advisors has helped hundreds of clients navigate complex tax situations, ensuring compliance, optimization, and peace of mind.
