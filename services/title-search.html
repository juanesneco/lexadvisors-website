Title Search

Services / Title Search

Lex Advisors provides comprehensive title search services to help clients identify potential issues and ensure clear property ownership before real estate transactions.

Our title search practice covers property history, ownership verification, lien searches, and encumbrance identification. We conduct thorough investigations to uncover any potential issues that could affect property ownership or value.

We assist with public record searches, ownership chain verification, lien and judgment searches, and title insurance preparation. Our team ensures that all title searches comply with Mexican property law and regulatory requirements.

Whether you're purchasing property, refinancing, or conducting due diligence, our title search services provide the comprehensive analysis and risk assessment you need to make informed decisions.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican property law, Lex Advisors has helped thousands of clients successfully identify and resolve title issues, ensuring secure property ownership and transaction success.
